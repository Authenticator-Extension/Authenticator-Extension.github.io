## Chrome

1. 打开`chrome://extensions`

2. 勾选右上角“开发者模式”

3. 点击“立即更新扩展”

![chrome](/assets/update-screenshots/chrome.png)

## Firefox

1. 打开`about:addons`

2. 点击右上角的齿轮

3. 点击“检查更新”

![firefox](/assets/update-screenshots/firefox.png)
